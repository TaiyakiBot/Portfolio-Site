Non-Designed Object

Butter Knife

I chose a butter knife as my non designed object as non-butter-specific knives are also able to cut through butter nearly just as well. Butter knives are not able to cut through anything other than butter, making it inefficient. Yet, it is still something I use and enjoy using on a daily basis despite it's drawbacks, and it is something I'll always have in my kitchen.
